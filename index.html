Why I like this topic

I like learning how to code because it feels personal. A blank screen can become a small digital world, a personal garden, a website, or a creative project.

I also like retro technology, anime, games, and personal creative projects because they mix logic with imagination. Old computers, glowing screens, strange little interfaces, and digital spaces inspire me because technology does not have to feel boring or corporate. It can feel personal, weird, useful, and alive.

Sometimes coding feels like filling an empty garden with words, images, links, colors, and small pieces of myself. It starts as nothing, then slowly becomes something I can share online.
